General Experience and Reflections

The most challenging part was working with other people. It was a hard thing for me, but something I loved with this experience is that I feel more confident than ever to work with others. What I enjoyed most is seeing what other people wanted, and tried to make it a reality. I do feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience, as now I know on a whim what I would need to complete what I have in my mind.
